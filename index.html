Welcome to the Realm of Inner Demons!

In this pixelated turn-based adventure, you'll embark on a journey to conquer the most formidable foes of all: your own inner demons!
From battling procrastination to facing off against self-doubt, every turn is a chance to overcome the obstacles holding you back.

So gear up and dive into the pixelated world as you strive to conquer the Realm of Inner Demons and emerge victorious!

INSTRUCTIONS :

Actions: You have three actions you can take during your turn:

  • Attack: Click the "Attack" button to attack the enemy. This will deal damage to the enemy and consume stamina. If your stamina is too low, you won't be able to attack.

  • Rest: Click the "Rest" button to rest and restore some stamina. This action doesn't deal damage to the enemy but allows you to regain stamina for future attacks.

  • Heal: Click the "Heal" button to heal your character. This action consumes stamina but restores your character's health.


Enemy Turn: After you take your turn, the enemy will attack you. The enemy's attacks will deal damage to your character's health.

Victory: If you defeat all the enemies without your character's health dropping to zero, you win the game.

Game Over: If your character's health drops to zero at any point during the game, it's game over.

Restart: You can restart the game at any time by clicking the "Restart" button on the victory or game over screens.
